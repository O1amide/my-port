How to Use SpeedClean

Get the best results with proper usage

1

Sort Your Clothes

Separate whites, colors, and delicates for best results

2

Pre-treat Stains

Apply SpeedClean directly to tough stains and let sit for 5 minutes

3

Measure Correctly

Use 1 capful for regular loads, 2 capfuls for heavily soiled items

4

Wash & Enjoy

Add to washing machine and enjoy fresh, clean clothes

Pro Tips

  • For hand washing, dissolve SpeedClean in water before adding clothes
  • Use warm water for better stain removal on white clothes
  • Don't overload your washing machine for best results
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from children
